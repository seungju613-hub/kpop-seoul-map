BTS SEOUL SPOT 📍

HYBE Back Gate Cafe Street

하이브 후문 카페거리
📍 Address: Yongsan-gu, Seoul (behind HYBE building)
🇰🇷 서울 용산구 하이브 후문 일대

The narrow street behind HYBE's building has become one of Seoul's most unique fan destinations. During BTS comebacks and member birthdays, this entire block transforms into a festival of fan-organized events.

Cupsleeve events, photo exhibitions, free goods distribution — cafes along this street partner with fan accounts to create immersive experiences. Bomnal Cafe is one of the permanent fixtures, hosting events for every major BTS occasion.

Even on quiet days, the area is worth visiting. You can see HYBE's headquarters up close and soak in the atmosphere of BTS's home base.
