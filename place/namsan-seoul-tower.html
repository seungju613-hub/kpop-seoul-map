BTS SEOUL SPOT 📍

Namsan Seoul Tower

남산서울타워
📍 Address: 105 Namsangongwon-gil, Yongsan-gu, Seoul
🇰🇷 서울 용산구 남산공원길 105

Namsan Seoul Tower is one of Seoul's most recognizable landmarks, sitting atop Namsan Mountain with 360-degree views of the entire city. It's featured BTS THE CITY media facade installations and has appeared in various BTS-related content.

The tower is famous for its "love lock" fence where couples attach padlocks with messages. The cable car ride up offers beautiful views, especially at sunset.

For BTS fans, it's a must-visit Seoul experience — and being in Yongsan-gu, it's close to HYBE and the neighborhoods where all seven members built their lives.
